B.M.S. College of Commerce & Management (BMSCCM) is run under the aegis of BMS Educational Trust, Bengaluru. The college is affiliated to Bangalore City University, Bengaluru and offers programs as per the prescribed syllabus of the university. BMSCCM is centrally located in the verdant green environs of V.V Puram, Basavanagudi Bengaluru. The college offers under-graduate courses in Commerce, Management and Computer Applications Programs and Executive Management Program for working professionals.
